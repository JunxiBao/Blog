Following my previous article, I decided to use some of my red envelope money to start my cryptocurrency investment journey. Since I've decided to enter the market, the security of my funds is my top priority.

There's a famous saying in the crypto space: "Not your keys, not your coins." To achieve true control over my assets, I needed a physically isolated, never-connected device to store my private keys. After comparing various hardware wallets on the market, Tangem successfully caught my attention.

Why Choose Tangem?

Compared to traditional hardware wallets with screens, Tangem's card-style design has several significant advantages:

  • No Seed Phrase Hassle: It doesn't require users to manually record and save complex seed phrases. As long as you have the card, you can make secure transactions.
  • Long Battery Life and Lifespan: Because it has no screen or battery, it doesn't need charging at all. Its simple physical structure also means a longer lifespan.
  • Flexible Backup Mechanism: I can activate multiple backup cards simultaneously. Even if I lose one, I can recover my assets using a backup card.

International Shipping and Initial Surprises (and Regrets)

Interestingly, this order was the first time in my life I paid directly using cryptocurrency (I paid in BTC). The package was shipped from Hong Kong and took about 10 days to arrive. Along the way, I also paid 75 RMB in customs duties at Ningbo Customs.

To be honest, I felt a slight sense of disappointment the moment I unboxed it. Looking at the official promotional images, I originally thought the cards were made of full aluminum alloy—after all, a single card costs over 200 RMB. However, upon receiving them, I found they were made of ordinary plastic. But on second thought, "looks" are just superficial; security is the core, and as long as it works well, that's what matters.

Smooth Activation Experience

The UI design of the Tangem App is excellent, and the entire activation process is smooth and highly modern. I only needed to use my phone's NFC feature to scan two cards in sequence, and the setup was completed in just a few minutes.

Adjusted Portfolio Allocation

Considering that I still want to pursue returns while keeping risks manageable, I slightly adjusted the portfolio allocation mentioned in my previous article based on my understanding of the market:

Asset Type Percentage Rationale
BTC (Bitcoin) 60% Absolute base position, aiming for stability.
ETH (Ethereum) 30% Balancing ecosystem value and growth potential.
RENDER / ONDO 10% (5% each) Positioning in AI computing and RWA sectors for higher elasticity.

Now, the hardware security is in place, and the portfolio strategy is clear. My journey of exploring the crypto world has officially set sail.

在上一篇文章之后，我决定动用一部分压岁钱开启我的加密货币投资之旅。既然决定入场，资金安全就是我的头等大事。

币圈有一句名言：“Not your keys, not your coins（不是你的密钥，就不是你的资产）”。为了实现真正的资产掌控，我需要一个物理隔离、永不联网的设备来存放私钥。在对比了市面上各类硬件钱包后，Tangem 成功吸引了我的注意。

为什么选择 Tangem？

相比于传统的带屏幕硬件钱包，Tangem 的卡片式设计有几个非常显著的优势：

  • 摆脱助记词烦恼： 它不需要用户手动记录和保存复杂的助记词，只要卡片在手，就能进行安全交易。
  • 长久续航与寿命： 因为没有屏幕和电池，它完全不需要充电，物理结构简单意味着使用寿命更长。
  • 备份机制灵活： 我可以同时激活多张备份卡，即便丢失其中一张，也能通过备用卡找回资产。

跨国物流与初见惊喜（及遗憾）

有趣的是，这笔订单是我人生中第一次直接使用加密货币进行支付（我支付了 BTC）。包裹从香港发出，大约 10 天送达，中间在宁波海关还缴纳了 75 元的税费。

说实话，开箱那一刻我有一丝落差。看着官方宣传图，我本以为这卡片是全铝合金材质的——毕竟单价也要 200 多元——结果到手发现是普通的塑料材质。不过转念一想，“颜值”只是表象，安全性才是核心，好用就行。

丝滑的激活体验

Tangem App 的 UI 设计非常出色，整个激活过程流畅且极具现代感。我只需要用手机的 NFC 功能依次感应两张卡片，短短几分钟就完成了设置。

调整后的仓位配置

考虑到我依然希望在风险可控的前提下追求收益，我根据市场理解微调了上一篇文章中提到的仓位分配：

资产类型 比例 逻辑说明
BTC (比特币) 60% 绝对底仓，追求稳健。
ETH (以太坊) 30% 兼顾生态价值与成长性。
RENDER / ONDO 10% (各5%) 布局 AI 算力与 RWA 赛道，博取更高弹性。

现在，硬件保障已经就位，仓位策略也已明确，我的加密世界探索之旅正式起航。
